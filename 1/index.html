How to Play Minesweeper

Clear the board without detonating any mines. Tiles will reveal numbers indicating how many mines are adjacent to that tile.

  • Left Click: Reveals a tile. If you reveal a mine, the game ends.
  • Right Click : Flags a tile as a potential mine. This helps you keep track of where you suspect mines are located.
  • Reset Button: Resets the game board.
  • Winning: You win by revealing all tiles that are not mines.
